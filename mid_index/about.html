About Me

My Ocean Journey

Hello! I'm Yu-Shen Chang, a passionate ocean enthusiast and creative web developer. Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by the mysterious depths of the ocean and the incredible life it holds.

This website is my personal dream space where I combine my love for marine biology with web development skills. Through interactive experiences and visual storytelling, I hope to share the beauty and wonder of the underwater world.

My Interests

When I'm not coding or exploring the web, you can find me:

  • Scuba diving in tropical waters
  • Photographing marine life
  • Reading about ocean conservation
  • Creating digital art inspired by the sea
  • Learning about coral reef ecosystems

Why the Ocean?

The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet, yet we've only explored about 5% of it. This vast unknown represents endless possibilities and mysteries waiting to be discovered. To me, the ocean symbolizes both tranquility and adventure, making it the perfect theme for my dream space.

Through this website, I aim to inspire others to appreciate and protect our precious marine ecosystems while showcasing the technical skills I've developed in web development.
